- Twemoji graphics are Creative Commons Attribution 4.0, not public domain/CC0.
- Google Noto Emoji SVG assets are Apache-2.0 licensed, not public domain/CC0.

No third-party emoji art, flag art, weapon art, or icon artwork is bundled.
